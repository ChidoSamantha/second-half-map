Wheel of Priorities

How content or satisfied am I today with how things are going in the different areas of my life?

This wheel is a snapshot of your life right now — not a test, not a scorecard. It's simply a way to see yourself more clearly across all the areas that matter most to you.

In the center of the wheel, 0 represents very little or no satisfaction. At the outer edge, 10 represents being fully satisfied. Rate each area based on where your time and energy are going right now — not where you think they should be.

Take a breath, be honest, and remember: there is no good or bad score, and "10" is not the goal. Allow it to reveal what is important to you now, what you would like to change, and what is already going well. You may also adjust any life area label to better reflect your life.

How to use it
1
Rate your contentment & satisfaction — drag the red marker (0–10) based on how content and satisfied you are in each area right now
2
Set your second half goal — drag the navy marker to where you want to be. Hint: start by thinking about where you want to be in 1 year
3
Toggle the view — switch between Where I am now, My second half goal, or Now + goal to see your full picture
4
Practice! Choose 1–2 life areas to focus on in the next 6–12 months — remember what is already working well that you can continue
Use this to help you choose your focus areas — then review and revise your wheel 2–3 times per year. Notice changes and make new goals.
